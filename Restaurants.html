Gua Bao 割包
Beef Noodle Soup 牛肉麵
Fried Rice 炒飯
Gua Bao is a dish that originated from China.
It is a bun that has been sliced in half, with meat sandwiched
inside the bun.
Beef Noodle Soup originated around Taiwan, and it
is one of the most popular dish in Taiwan. Beef noodle soup
is usually made of red braised beef, beef broth, vegetables,
and Chinese noodles.
Fried Rice is a dish that is usually made by stir-frying rice,
along with ther ingredients, such as eggs, vegetables,
seafood, and meat. It is often found in Taiwan, and it is a very
popular dish, along with many others.
